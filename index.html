Explore Our Services

vCISO

A vCISO service is a cybersecurity consulting service that provides organizations with access to a virtual chief information security officer (vCISO) on a part-time or contract basis. A vCISO is a cybersecurity expert who can help organizations develop and implement effective cybersecurity strategies and manage risks. A vCISO service is a cost-effective and flexible option for organizations, providing access to expert cybersecurity guidance and leadership without the need for a full-time CISO.

Adversary Emulation

Adversary emulation is a cybersecurity technique that involves simulating the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) of real-world attackers to test and improve an organization's defenses. During Purple Teaming engagements, we collaborate with your cybersecurity and incident response teams, in which we simulates an attack and your team responds to it, in order to assess and improve the organization's overall security posture.

Digital Forensics and Incident Response

Digital forensics and incident response (DFIR) is the practice of collecting and analyzing digital evidence after a cyber attack or incident to determine the cause and identify any data or information that may have been compromised. It is a crucial part of an organization's cybersecurity strategy, as it helps organizations understand and respond to cyber attacks and prevent future incidents.

Security Assesments

Security assessments are processes that involve evaluating an organization's security posture and identifying any weaknesses or vulnerabilities that could be exploited by cyber attackers. They can be performed on various aspects of an organization's security infrastructure, including its networks, systems, applications, and data.

Cyber Expert Witness

A cybersecurity expert witness is a professional with specialized knowledge in cybersecurity who provides testimony in legal proceedings about technical matters related to cyber attacks, data breaches, and other cyber incidents. They may be retained by legal counsel to provide expert testimony in cases involving cybersecurity-related disputes or to assist in investigations.
